WELCOME TO THE ROYAL CERAMIC CLUB

Welcome to the Royal Ceramic Club. Ceramic arts are among the most eagerly awaited by art collectors worldwide, and porcelain has risen to prominence with its one-of-a-kind process that produces beautiful designs in vivid colors beneath the surface of a piece of porcelain.     SOCIETY

    You will become a member of the Society by purchasing a vase. There are four levels of society and each with its own set of benefits. You will be placed in one of four levels based on the rarity of the vase, which is determined at random, with Earthenware being the least rare and Bone China being the most rare.

  • Earthenware — the longest established form of pottery. This is the softest type of pottery, being opaque and non-vitreous.
  • Stoneware — it has stone-like qualities. It is durable, elegant, and versatile.
  • Porcelain — exquisite translucence. Its durability, softness, and beauty are unparalleled.
  • Bone China — the toughest type. Premium material with remarkable physical strength and delicate appearance.
